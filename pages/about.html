Meet Line By Lili Lamber

The story of the brand is actually the story of a dream

A ready-to-wear fashion brand that combines respect for craftsmanship and premium-quality fabrics. Offering unconventional, well-tailored, and sustainable women's clothing.

The story of the brand is actually the story of a dream that took a long time to mature and finally became a trigger to put everything on the line - an attempt to turn a passion into a life idea. We were willing to take the risk, no matter the outcome.

Our clothing is inspired by travels, people, and experiences. They represent the culmination of a long search for the essence of beauty and style. However, we believe that this definition will continue to evolve and grow with us, just like the accessories to our dresses. A woman who chooses Line is attuned to current trends but also conscious of the challenges posed by a changing world. She aims to look stunning without the hassle of lengthy preparations. She values comfort and needs garments that adapt with her - perfect for a business meeting in the morning, and later, paired with trainers, ready for navigating the urban landscape.

Our collections are crafted in Poland by a team of meticulously selected professionals. We use the finest natural materials to produce our garments. Many of these materials come from Italy, the mecca of high-quality fabrics and fashion, but we also collaborate with selected Polish manufacturers.
